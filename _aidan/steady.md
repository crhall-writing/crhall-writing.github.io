title: steady
date: 2026-06-16

I'm doing my best to hold myself steady  
to focus on everything I need to  
to focus on it--for you  
but my leg bobs and my lids are heavy  

and then I smell the Boulder air  
and realize it smells just like you did  
with so much pressure against that thin  
fabric, and I'm drunk on the way you take me there  

I love water, maybe too much  
and it tastes just like your shoulder, glistening  
with steaming streams slipping between, listening  
to the deepest crevasses of your throat, and such  

overwhelming amounts of you filling every place  
I love whenever you warm my home  
and I'm with you whenever alone  
I'm amazed at the way you take up space  

I'll do everything I can to keep it  
to cherish you, holding you close  
because you deserve the greatest love you've ever known  
and you do nothing but make me see it
