date: 2026-06-27

I read something  
by someone, at some point,  
and the author said that the most beautiful moment  
in life happens just as you begin to think  
I have the capacity to love this person  
and they have it also for me  

and I remember that exactly  

over the phone, saying I’ve never known  
any man I’d like to stay up all night  
in conversation, immersed in each other  
it’s always been feeling awake, alive  
with someone whose soul seems to quickly die  
and I listened to the most gorgeous of descriptions  
of your love for Maurice and Katia  
and my vision blurred and I softened  
into the soft bed in that guest room  
and when I watched orange and blue come into view  
over apartment buildings beyond the panes  
I somehow knew, and maybe we felt the same,  
that this would always be what we should do
