I feel like I'm counting down seconds  
milliseconds, microseconds, nanoseconds  
until I see you  
I feel like three thousand gifts  
are balled into one  
and you are the only gift I'll ever want  

I can't wait to feel you against my back  
your arms pulling me in firmly again  
never, *ever* let me go  

I remember, the greatest feeling I've ever felt  
being enveloped by you in that hammock  
pretending I wanted to get away  
embarrassed, and you only pulled me in tighter  

I felt so incredibly safe  
like I would never, ever fall out  
and I didn't even realize  
you were at the very edge  
like you always are, in both of our beds  
but you always make sure I first have room  

I will never be more loved  
and I never want to be  
because no one will ever feel  
like you do holding me
