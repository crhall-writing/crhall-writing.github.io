title: seen
date: 2026-04-19
layout: poem
---
have you seen the sprawling green  
light bouncing between the dew and haze above  

have you been taken prisoner by the awe  
of a tractor so many times your size  

have you dreamed of dancing and jumping  
and singing off-key lyrics together in a field?   

have you screamed those songs out rolled-down windows  
and smelled the manure and must of horse or cow or something else?    

have you seemed different one day  
like some repeated inevitability was smashed into a million pieces?   

have you gleaned what I’ve been meaning?  
and will you take my hand and come with me through it all?  

and if you daydream about the same things  
that’ll always be enough for me
