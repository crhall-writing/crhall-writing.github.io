date: 2026-06-22

---
you know, I wanted to write about  
that guy who spotted the Titanic’s fatal iceberg  
but what really is gripping me  
is this girl  

she’s blonde, maybe some would say fake  
but she makes me feel so inherently safe  
I want to support her, make her feel wanted and deserved  
the way I now am, somehow  

I can’t believe I’m lucky enough  
to want to share my light with a stranger  
I bet she’s happy—I bet she’s fulfilled  
and she does what she needs to earn her will  

and I’m happy for her  
I wish her the life that’s laid in front of me  
without judgement, without ego  
you don’t need ego when you’re whole
