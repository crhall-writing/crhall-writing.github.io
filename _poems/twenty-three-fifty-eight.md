title: twenty-three-fifty-eight
date: 2026-04-13

---
“are you well read?”  
it’s twenty-three-fifty-eight  
is it not?  
and this one will be a cacophony  

“are you well read?”  
it won’t have your precious pentameter  
nor rhyme, but  
by God will it have reason  

“are you well read?”  
and if you wanted to join me  
in the endeavor  
you know you can  

“are you well read?”  
but I think at—what is it?  
zero-zero-oh-one  
you’re fast asleep  

“are you well read?”  
and I s’pose you won’t know  
what it’s like to sit across from me    
spouting thoughts at zero-zero-oh-three  

“are you well read?”  
fuck—of course I am!  
you *know* I’ve always been  
but what I’ve read isn’t what you mean  

“are you well read?”  
if you ask me once more I’ll fucking scream  
you’ve never listened to a word that spilled outside  
the neat walls of a bounded ream  

“are you well read?”  
so I know those shallow ears  
won’t bend a hair to my quiz:  
“are you well lived?”
