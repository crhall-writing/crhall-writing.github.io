what do you mean you  
need to take life so seriously?  

you mean you didn’t finish calc III  
at age 16   

get a postdoc offer in your favorite city  
nor complain about your circumstance    

I can’t ever tell you to come with me  
that’s unfair, I agree   

I’m not more experienced  
I’m just a little luckier at the end of the night  

I don’t know if I’ll meet someone like you  
ever again  

what’s strange is there’s nothing I can hold onto  
that differentiates you from any other men  

but for whatever reason, whatever spite   
I don’t think this feeling will happen twice
