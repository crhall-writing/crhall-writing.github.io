---
title: green boots
date: 2026-03-15
layout: poem
---
“the base is the other way”, he’ll say    
bare rock invisible under a white blanket  
that will cover the deathbed of so many  
who look with eyes, blinded  

whether by the shards of harsh air  
that will not become more oxygenated for your pleasure  
it won’t save you even in your dying breath  
and your eyes will never adjust to the sun  

brighter than you could ever imagine  
even if you manage an escape, by the tip of your skin  
now frostbitten, red that will never return to flesh    
it’ll instead die, rot, and fall off  

like our friends had, from that tiny gap  
taking them to a new crevasse  
or river down below, the unclipped crampon  
being all that’s left, and yesterday the path was whole  

his voice will echo out the small cave  
peacefully propped against the last escape  
he’d get from the unforgiving breeze  
and all you’ll see--are boots of green
