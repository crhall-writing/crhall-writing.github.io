title: vain
date: 2026-04-12

---
I look at you like a broom looks to dust, now  
he’s so vain he’d probably think this poem’s about him  
I know that truth well enough at this point.  

you haven’t seen enough white walls  
to write like your life depends on it  
you haven’t seen the ugliest part   

you haven’t seen the worst of love  
nor fallen to your knees in exhaust  
I know you better than myself  

and I loved so much  
the carefree and thick hair  
that had blown in the breeze  

younger than me by some years  
now decades younger in life  
I can’t believe myself sometimes  

for thinking there was more there  
than some blank canvas I could paint  
to give some wisdom, some experience  

and maybe in time, you’ll do it yourself  
but from whatever version I knew myself  
you’ll wait for another to do it for you  

and even though I thought you’d be my soulmate  
the one I had was the better of you both  
he at least knew; he’d reap whatever he sowed
