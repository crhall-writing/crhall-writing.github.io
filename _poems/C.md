title: C
date: 2026-05-24

---
I hope we’re friends forever  
you who so clearly sometimes  
shoots a shot that makes sense to shoot  
and pretends it never happened when I do  

and you told everyone  
my voice was the most beautiful thing you’ve ever heard  
no one’s ever done anything like that for me  
but knowing you has made me raise that bar  

and you told me you were so happy  
that you loved him the way I might one day  
“I fucking like this guy,” you’d say  
and I know you’d help me build it, no matter what  

I’m not dealing with this move so well  
as I’m sure, at this point, you can probably tell  
I can’t just meet you at our spot  
when I’m in the mountains and you’re in the Plains  

but you’ll always be C  
and I’ll always be Cece  
two sides of the same coin  
though I can be bad at showing
