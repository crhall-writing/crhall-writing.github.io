title: anticipation
date: 2026-03-04
layout: poem
---
it’s whatever happens just before  
that makes the real impact  

whatever excitement I felt just before it entered    
whatever breath I took just before it hit  
whatever thoughts I thought leading to it happening  

they were all so much better  

in those moments I saw your glasses reflecting dim lights  
where I noticed your eyes were green    
as they stared back at me  
when everyone else talked over the two of us  

we didn’t need any reason to speak    
my heart raced just as much  
as it did knowing what you feel   

but maybe, too  
the fear of leaving  
of going somewhere new  
is just as fleeting  

maybe I’ll arrive  
and think    
this was a lot more scary  
in my mind
