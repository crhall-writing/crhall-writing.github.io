title: monopoly
date: 2026-06-27

---
look who I have now  
not someone who will try to sit there  
convincing me of how wrong they think I am  
someone who believes in me unequivocally  
instead of leaving the second something funner comes along  

do you feel bad? mistreated?  
I think that you should  
because you were  
I pity you  
for the power you tried to hold over me  

you lost it  
everything that gave you meaning  
while I sustained my meaning alone  
and now I have something that doesn’t monopolize me  
he lets me grow in ways you’ll never know
