the ice is cut just the same  
whether rain re-polishes the plane  
or the Zamboni in Loring Park bears the blame  

and I suppose you wouldn’t know  
I never got the chance to skate  
in Rockefeller Plaza  

but that’s only as fleeting   
as the sprinkled snow in May  
it’ll come back soon, just as it leaves  

and I’ll miss autumn seen for miles ahead  
no snow-capped leviathan swallowing the frame   
I’ll need to aim toward the top’f the world instead
