when I tell you my woes  
or regrets    
passions or loves  
will your response  
be to ask  
or to tell?  

I want both  
I want them all  
I want to be laid on a blanket  
in the grass  


since you should know  
how scared I am of ticks  
and if you pinned my hands  
to the sides of my neck  

I hope you’ll use that  
use me  
to do good  
for you and for us
