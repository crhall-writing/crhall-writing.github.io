---
title: the road taken
date: 2026-03-15
layout: poem
---
“she’s just like the other girls”  
a million roads fork in the center  
and branch and diverge  
like phylogenetic trees, slender  

they become, like they might run  
dry as the rivers between all of my homes  
and now that I’ve found another one  
I’d sit down with those who’ve grown  

from lives just as different and just as same  
your pain and mine form strings between  
every heart that aches with none to blame  
we’re not dissimilar, you and me
