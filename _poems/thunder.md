and the thunder will break through the atmosphere  
it will—whether you answer  
and it does, beautifully so  
pour through the eve’s glow  

and I’ll continue to experience more  
than they ever thought one should  
because what has ever cured  
all that  bursting clouds could  

for is my desire truly suffering?  
when everything is gorgeous and saturated?  
my instincts are just the offering  
to a storm I’ll heed, infatuated
