---
title: Solomon is dying
date: 2026-04-17
layout: poem
---
close your eyes and feel it pound   
the three bleeds within your skull   
vertebrae cracked and torn   
since the rock betrayed your foot   

all eyes to the shadow of your form  
cast to three times its size when night would fall  

Solomon is dying  
a bruised rib and bleeding brain  
but he died so many years ago  
his shadow stained the wall
