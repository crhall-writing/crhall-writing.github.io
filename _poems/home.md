title: home
date: 2026-04-09
layout: poem
---
you know, I never knew what it was  
until very recently  
to look at the features on someone’s face  
and feel warm, like there is some home  

that doesn’t cause hyperventilation  
as soon as you go back  
no panic to be found  
and how lucky it is  

to know people   
who make you feel this way  
I don’t care how late it came
