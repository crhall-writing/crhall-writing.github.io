maybe I did it for some control  
or to feel something  
anything  
aside from the emptiness
of wanting what will never come  
and would never come  

maybe I wanted to know  
I stood up for the hope of more  
I put my foot down  
maybe it was on the neck of mediocrity  
to make myself feel deeper than I am  

for years ago this felt different  
and I was so different for being so okay  
but lately I’ve felt the weight  
of being who I really am  

I now you won’t care  
you’ll shrug those numb shoulders 
and go on with your day   
and your week  
and your month and year  

and the future will be how   
it already would’ve been   
I never impacted it in any way  
you don’t know any more from me  
and I never really knew you  

the world spins at the same speed  
and my world is forever un-based  
I feed some heaviness, yes, in my chest  
maybe some day I’ll cry  
but I don’t feel enough to yet
