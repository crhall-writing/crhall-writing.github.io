title: petals
date: 2026-05-15

and down each corridor  
there lies a new surprise  
and each one will breed nothing more  
than fine details of your eyes  

as they try to find something in mine  
but they’ve gone dark like I always do  
and you should know this time  
that I’ve left the hall for good  

and maybe it’s good—for our best  
you’ll pick apart some some other  
the youngest, onto the next  
promising and fine with your hover  

still, and maybe that one will be timeless  
unlike me whose glass ran daft of sand   
I do nothing but peel away petals and find it  
simple to blow them from my hand
