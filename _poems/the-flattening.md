---
title: the flattening
date: 2026-05-24
layout: poem
---
I’m free of you, for now  
and forever  
I’ll never again have to swallow myself  
with shoulders collapsing  
mind flattening  
into nothing more than the ruins of Alexandria.  

you’ll always be that way, I’m guessing  
to get the reference  
memorized, echoing in the hollow  
and you’ll go on, so it goes  
without a hint of understanding.
