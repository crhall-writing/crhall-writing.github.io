the elegy to Keats  
that called Adonais  
	
I know you know it  
and if you didn’t  
I’d have no fascination of you  

but---oh, maybe  
now is the time  
that might have been warranted  
so long ago  

and I’d like to think  
there will be someone higher  
waiting for me  
but there’s a reason this is an elegy  

I’ll never stop loving you  
and I fear you know that truth  

and not because I can’t come to terms  
with my heart and liver and breast  

it’s the fact that  
I wasn’t ever a draw enough    
to start the conversation
