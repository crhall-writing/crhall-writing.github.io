title: latch
date: 2026-05-24
layout: poem
---
you just don’t get it, do you?  
the entire world could be yours if you’d just  
fucking keep your giant, empty head down  
we could have something nice for once in a year  

but I must outsource, so much  
to escape your obsessive ways  
you listen each time a door opens  
and I’ve never had five minutes without an entrance  

why is it, that the more one pulls away  
you take it as invitation to latch and stay?
