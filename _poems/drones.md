try tobogganing  
under lost gods hovering  

they could end up wherever they want  
why do they always stay above us?  

no matter what we do  
how many stones I steal from you  

no matter how many eggs we lose-ta  
the next looming skua  

no matter the time of endless day  
we go to forage for prey  

they’ll buzz faintly, somewhere up there  
while problems of we are level with our stare  

the shining substance, flying tall  
might as well be a disco ball  

so toboggan on  
and with the noise you can dance along
