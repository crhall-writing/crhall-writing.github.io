---
title: violins
date: 2026-04-20
layout: poem
---
hear the violins shriek  
as your pupils dilate with my own  
meet me there  
the little sliver of mud along the river  

and we can skip the flattest stones we find  
along the water I thought would be calmer  
and maybe your heart will skip as mine  

and I’ll finally get those chances I never had before  
suppose I asked you every pressing question  
that’s been on my mid since the beginning  
would it make any difference this time?  

or would you hold me hostage again  
to your whims that work not  
with the scarcity of time?  

if we could lay it flat  
as those stones  
imagine the possibilities  

but if you can’t even hold a conversation  
about inevitability  
and you must collapse it all down to some  
hollowed representation of what could’ve been  

I’d rather stroll those slivers of mud alone
